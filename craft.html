The Process

Engineering Style

From deconstructing luxury bags to re-shaping retail fits—this is the artistry behind the name.

The Carvela Transformation

Welcome Phuti is a master of structural repurposing. Watch as he meticulously deconstructs luxury materials to create the signature Black Citizen caps—fusing leather heritage with modern street elegance.

01. Anatomical Fit

We believe clothing should follow the body, not the other way around. Every measurement is taken to ensure a natural, sharp silhouette that enhances your confidence.

02. Master Alterations

Don't settle for "baggy" shop clothes. We unpick, re-cut, and re-sew retail garments to give them the exclusive, custom-tailored look of high-end fashion.

03. Denim Engineering

Building trousers and jeans from raw denim requires heavy-duty precision. Our hand-stitched denim is engineered for both durability and a luxury finish.

"A tailor fixes clothes; a master builds a citizen's identity. We engineer the fit so you can own the room."
— Welcome Phuti, Founder
